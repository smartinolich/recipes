Ingredients:
 - 6-7 Russet potatoes
 - 4 tablespoons butter
 - 2 teaspoons Slap Ya Mamas (or any Cajun seasoning)
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon pepper
 - 1 teaspoon paprika
 - Louisiana hot sauce to taste (or your preferred hot sauce)

Directions:
 1. Cut the potatoes into cubes. 
 2. In a large skillet, add the butter and heat on medium-high heat until melted.
 3. Add the cubed potatoes to the skillet and cover.
 4. Cook for approximately 15 minutes, stirring often to coat the potatoes in butter and seasoning.
 5. After 15 minutes, or when the potatoes are cooked through, serve!
